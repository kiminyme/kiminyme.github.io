About Me

I have been teaching in higher education for more than 20 years, first in French, and then in the field of Computer Science.

I have a Bachelors degree in French from Davidson College, as well as a Masters degree in French Linguistics from Indiana University-Bloomington.

After leaving grad school, I entered the field of computer repair, earned an A+ certifcation, then became a professional corporate trainer.

I am currently an Assistant Professor in Computer Science at a small, private university in Louisville, Kentucky. I am completing the MET in order to specialize in development of online courses, as well as to improve my own use of technology in the classes I currently teach.
