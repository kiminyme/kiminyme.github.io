Evaluation

Use the rubric below to do a self-evaluation of how well you completed this project. You may also use the rubric as a guide to corrections you may want to make before the assignment is graded.

 

Excellent

Fair

Poor

Part 1:
Minimum Requirements
(10 points)

Each of the minimum system requirements were identified appropriately, based on the friend’s needs.

8-10 points

Most of the minimum system requirements were identified appropriately, based on the friend’s needs.

4-7 points

Few of the friend’s needs were identified appropriately.

0-3 points

Part 2:
Discussion
(5 points)

There were posts to the discussion board comparing your findings with those of at least two classmates.

4-5 points

There was at least one post to the discussion board comparing your findings with only one classmate.

1-3 points

There is no evidence that your findings were compared to those of your classmates.

0 points

Part 3:
System Analysis
(20 points)

You identified two compatible systems, with all specifications listed for both systems.

15-20 points

You identified two compatible systems, but many specifications were missing.

7-14 points

You identified only one compatible system, or the analysis was incomplete.

0-6 points

Conclusion
(10 points)

You presented a system that meets her needs, with a good explanation of why that system was better than the other system considered.

8-10 points

You chose a system to recommend, but the choice was not well justified.

4-7 points

You failed to identify a system that would meet her needs.

0-3 points
