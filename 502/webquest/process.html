The Process

Step 1: Do research and complete a form to identify what your friend needs in a computer.

Click HERE to open and save a Word document that can be used to collect this information. You will save the file and submit the completed file to an online discussion board.

Step 2: Compare your results with those of your classmates.

After completing the form, go to the class discussion board for this assignment and create a new post. Attach a copy of your saved form to the new post, and include a brief summary of your results.

Once your form has been posted, look at the forms submitted by at least two other students. Reply to those posts with comments about differences you see between your conclusions and theirs, and/or ask questions about how they came to conclusions that are different from yours.

You may change your answers for this part of the exercise if you wish after reading your classmatesâ€™ posts. If you wish to submit a revised version, you can submit it as an attachment to a reply to your original post.

Step 3: Use the Web to find two systems that meet your friend's needs, based on the requirements you have already established.

Good starting points would be:

  • BestBuy (www.bestbuy.com)
  • TigerDirect (www.tigerdirect.com)
  • NewEgg (www.newegg.com)
  • Fry's (www.frys.com)

Step 4: Document your findings for step 3, and present a conclusion about which system you would recommend.

Click HERE to open and save a Word document that can be used to collect this information. Submit your completed file to the dropbox for this assignment.

Step 5: Review the rubric that will be used to grade the assignment, to determine how well you did in completing the assignment.
