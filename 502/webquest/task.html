Task Overview

A friend of yours wants your help. She has decided that her old computer is too slow, and that it is time to get a new computer. She wants you to help her choose a computer that will meet her needs now and in the future.

She wants to be able to do the following tasks with her computer:

  • Bring the computer with her to class so that she can use it to take notes or perform other tasks that the teacher might ask the class to do.
  • Use Skype or Google+ to have video chats with her old high school friends back home.
  • Connect a high definition monitor or TV to the computer so that she can work with two monitors at once.
  • Play computer games in her spare time, especially popular, high-quality video games that have realistic graphics, like Tomb Raider or Skyrim.

She is also on a budget, so she doesn't want to spend more than $1,000 for the entire system, including not only the computer, but also a mouse and other equipment that she might need.

For this task, you will complete the following steps:

  1. Use a form to identify the type of computer she needs, and research the minimum required components for what she intends to do.
  2. Compare your results to those of other students.
  3. Use the Web to reseach computer systems that will meet her needs.
  4. Create a document that will outline two possible systems for her to choose from, with details about why each system will meet her needs.
  5. Make a recommendation of the system that would most suit your friend's needs, based on your analysis.
