Virtual Computer Tour

Welcome to the insides of your computer! This tour will give you an up-close and personal view of parts most users don't see.

The first stop will be the motherboard--the backbone of the computer.

Next, we will look at RAM--Random Access Memory. This is the computer's short term memory location.

From there, we will look at the CPU--the central processing unit. This is the brain of the computer.

Our last stop will be the hard drive, where most of your data is stored for future use.
