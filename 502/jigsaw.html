EDTECH 502: The Internet for Educators

This exercise is designed for students in a first-year, university-level Computer Concepts course.

Storage Options

Users today have a wide range of choices when it comes to saving files that they may want to use again in the future.

For this activity, you will be divided into four separate groups. Each group will focus on one type of storage media, as indicated in the table below.

Directions

Within each group, you will provide a short overview of the assigned storage option. The overview should describe the technology used to store data, examples of storage options that use the media, a list of advantages that media type has over the other three media types, and a list of disadvantages.

At the end of the activity, each group will share the information with the rest of the class.
